Lagstream

The ultimate network optimizer for Valorant and other games

What is Lagstream?

Lagstream is a powerful tool designed to reduce ping, improve connection stability, and provide a smoother gaming experience, especially in games like Valorant.

Features

DNS Flushing: Clears your DNS cache for faster connection.

Registry Tweaks: Optimizes TCP/IP settings for reduced latency.

VPN Integration: Connects to fast VPN servers to reduce routing issues.
